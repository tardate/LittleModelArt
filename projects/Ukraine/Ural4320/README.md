# #xxx Ural-4320

When "friends" take you for a ride... the Ural-4320 serving with the Armed Forces of Ukraine. An old but still decent 1:72 kit from ICM.

![Build](./assets/Ural4320_build.jpg?raw=true)

## Notes

### The Kit

[URAL-4320 Military Truck of the Armed Forces of Ukraine ICM No. 72708 1:72](https://www.scalemates.com/kits/icm-72708-ural-4320--1463809)
is the 2023 boxing of a 1990's tooling.

I purchased the kit from
[hobby.dn.ua](https://www.hobby.dn.ua/en/icm-icm-72708-ural-4320-armed-forces-ukraine-p-107828.html)
for €10.86 (Aug-2023)

See [instructions](./assets/72708-instructions.pdf)

![72708-kit-box](./assets/72708-kit-box.jpg?raw=true)

### Paint Scheme

Schemes:

1. УРАЛ-4320, Збройні сили України URAL-4320, Armed Forces of Ukraine
